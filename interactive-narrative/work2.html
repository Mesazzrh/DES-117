A Poster for the Autumn Moon Festival

This poster was designed to celebrate the 2023 Chinese Autumn Moon Festival, a vibrant cultural event held in San Francisco Chinatown. The festival, known for its colorful lanterns, mooncakes, and community gatherings, reflects the rich heritage and traditions of the Mid-Autumn Festival.

Using Adobe Photoshop, I combined royalty-free images, a harmonious color palette, and traditional Chinese motifs to create a poster that captures the festive spirit. The design prominently features the essential event details—date, location, and key highlights—while evoking a sense of celebration and togetherness.

Tools & Techniques:

  • Software: Adobe Photoshop
  • Design Elements: Royalty-free photography, traditional patterns, and typography
  • Focus: Engaging visuals that resonate with the festival’s cultural and celebratory essence
